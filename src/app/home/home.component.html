IPRO is the first ontology of the genre of Italian Progressive Rock. Its features are:

  • highly interconnected individuals
  • easy navigation
  • focus on studio albums
  • focus on instruments and members
  • focus on genres influences

🎤🎼 Navigate by band

🎶📝 Navigate by genre

🎸🎧 Navigate by instrument/production role

👨🎷 Navigate by musician

IPRO is a university project of the course Modellazione concettuale per il Web Semantico of Università degli Studi di Torino, Department of Computer Science.
